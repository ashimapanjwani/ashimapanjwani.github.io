With every book that I read, I make it a point to mark certain lines or paragraphs which I think are important or which have had a great impact on me. Afterwards, I copy all of these in a single place for future reference. By doing so, I can always go back and see the important parts of a particular book at a glance, without having to read it all over again.
You can learn more about this technique of reading and note-taking here.
